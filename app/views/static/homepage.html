Welcome to promp_!


Need a dynamic drawing prompt? A unique story idea to springboard from? promp_ was designed to brainstorm creative outlet prompts for you, and will happily add your input to its databanks to create even more crazy prompts!

How to Bake a Prompt

Ingredients
  • Characters: The 'who?' - the primary actor in your scene.
  • Tactics: The 'how?' - the means the character uses.
  • Goals: The 'what?' - the immediate reason for character action.
  • Quests: The 'why?' - the long-term reason for character action.


Combine all ingredients and mix well! What you end up with is a mad-libs style creative prompt that you're sure to love. Or maybe not. Who cares! Just generate a new prompt, remix your current prompt, or add new ingredients for the generator to work with. And have fun!
